Match Preview: Kerala Blasters FC vs NorthEast United FC

Following a memorable home win, where the Blasters secured all three points in a hard-fought 2-1 victory against East Bengal FC, the Club’s focus will now shift to continuing that momentum on the road. The Blasters will look to sharpen both offensive creativity and defensive solidity as they aim to extend their strong start to the season.NorthEast United FC:NorthEast United FC are coming off a close 3-2 defeat against Mohun Bagan Super Giant and will be determined to put in a solid performance in front of their home crowd. The Blasters will have to be prepared for a spirited challenge, as NorthEast United look to get back to winning ways.Key Players to Watch: Kerala Blasters FC

  1. Sachin Suresh:

    After his stellar performance in the previous match, the Kerala Blasters goalkeeper will be crucial in denying NorthEast’s attack and maintaining a strong defensive foundation.

  2. Noah Sadaoui:

    The versatile forward scored an equalizer against East Bengal and will be a significant threat in the attacking third with his pace and finishing ability.

  3. Kwame Peprah:

    With his decisive late goal in the last match, Peprah will be keen to continue his scoring form and make an impact in the offensive setup.

  4. Jesús Jiménez:

    Jiménez scored a crucial goal against Punjab FC, showcasing his ability to find the back of the net when it matters most. His presence will be vital as the Blasters face NorthEast.Where to WatchThe match will be broadcast live on Sports18, JioCinema, Asianet Plus, and OneFootball at 19:30 IST.
